step-ca / OIDC SSH cert integration (#133): register a step-ca CA
in Keys → Certificate authorities, sign in via OIDC in your browser,
and Haven mints a fresh Ed25519 keypair + cert against it. Lazy
renewal at connect time refreshes expiring certs; a daily background
check fires a notification when one's about to expire. Servers
presenting a host cert signed by a registered CA skip TOFU when the
cert is valid and the hostname is in its principals.

CA registration moved from Settings to the Keys tab — credentials
live with credentials. The Add/Edit CA dialog grows "Import from
file" for the root cert (PEM/DER) and SSH host CA pubkey (.pub).

SSH: replay PTY size on reconnect to fix corruption after WiFi hops
(#120). i18n: Korean translations (#146).
